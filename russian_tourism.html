Why visit russia

For many, a Russian vacation might not seem to be the obvious holiday choice. It s true that Russia still has a lot of developing to do, but the rough edges shouldn t stop you from appreciating the countless unique diamonds that should make travel to Russia a top contender for any serious traveler s bucket-list.

East-meets-West Culture

Apart from Turkey, Russia is the only nation in the world that encompasses both the Asian and European continents. This unparalleled transcontinental positioning provides a vantage point from which both sides of the world can be appreciated and examined in all their many merits and faults. Visitors to Russia will find that while the comforts of western society such as coffee shops with free WiFi and 24/7 supermarkets are widespread, the Cyrillic characters, along with Russias many other idiosyncrasies provide that perfect window into the East. On a visit to Russia, you would quickly recognize the scenes depicted on the murals in St Basil s cathedral; yet the lavish, ostentatious, showering of gold and vibrant colors are so Eastern in feeling.

Architecture

Each brick or weather-worn plank that form the foundation of Russias many architectural masterpieces tells a thousand stories. From the first, multi-domed, Byzantine churches to the over-the-top, Empire-style palaces (whose sheer excess would ironically spell the end of the Russian Empire); there is a mind-boggling array of architectural wonders to explore and learn the history of. Which is why a vacation in Russia is as much an education as it is a holiday.

Food

It s a shame that Russias fantastic culinary culture has yet to be discovered by people outside the Slavic-block. Nourishing, yet served with a twist of sophistication, Russias traditional dishes strike the perfect balance between healthy and hearty. While the soups, syrniki and syelodka-pod-shuba (herring-under-fur coat) give a visit to Russia its flavor; it s the drinks that set the tone in this land of mysticism and sincere friendship. Sample all that you can on your Russian vacation.

Winston Churchill once said that Russia is a riddle wrapped in a mystery inside an enigma.” and we agree. When you travel to Russia, you will see for yourself that it is a country like no other. It s impossible to capture the essence of this huge land in a few words it has to be seen, alive and kicking, in all its perplexity.
